About Dakia

We are a small, focused engineering team building practical tools for data transformation and .NET modernization.

Our mission

Many teams still rely on manual Excel work, ad-hoc scripts and legacy systems just to deliver simple outputs. We created Dakia to make these workflows repeatable, observable and easy to maintain.

Our background is in enterprise .NET development, integration projects and data-heavy applications. We combine that experience into a product that feels lightweight yet powerful.

• Replacing fragile one-off scripts
• Migrating .NET systems to modern stacks
