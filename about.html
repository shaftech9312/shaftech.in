About ShafTech

ShafTech builds practical, lightweight engineering tools that simplify data processing, automation, and modernization. Our flagship product, Dakia, is designed to remove complexity from everyday data workflows.

Our Mission

Many organizations still depend on manual Excel operations, inconsistent scripts, legacy ETL tools, and fragile workflows that are hard to scale or maintain.

At ShafTech, we created Dakia to turn these processes into automated, reliable, and repeatable transformations — without the overhead of heavy enterprise systems.

Our experience spans .NET development, data-driven applications, integrations, and automation pipelines. We turn that experience into tools that feel simple on the outside and powerful on the inside.
